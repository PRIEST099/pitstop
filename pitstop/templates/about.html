About PitStop

My Journey

PitStop was born out of a personal need to streamline car maintenance. The idea struck me when I faced recurring issues with my vehicle but struggled to keep track of the services and maintenance schedules. I realized there was a gap in the market for a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that could manage all vehicle-related services efficiently.

This project is also my Portfolio Project for Holberton School, showcasing my skills in web development and design.
